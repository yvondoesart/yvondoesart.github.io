About

Greetings and salutations! My name is Yvon Lu, and I am a young Vietnamese American artist located in NYC, NY. I’ve been drawing since childhood, but I fell in love with charcoal during a class called New York City as the Muse. The medium was addictive, fun, and encouraged me to push the boundaries of the monochrome. I love pushing values to the extremes and relying on contrast to tell a complete story. My work also often invokes charcoal textures to communicate themes and motifs. I am primarily interested in exploring the uncanny, the unsettling, and the candid, moments that don’t demand attention yet linger in the mind. Through the subjects of urban life, biology, and animalia, I utilize charcoal, ink, and other mediums to inspire viewers to feel and react.
